The New York Times
All the News Fit to Print

Unlimited access to NYT.com, the historical archive back to 1851, Cooking, Games, Wirecutter, The Athletic, and the inEducation site — plus the full suite of NYT apps.

How to Register
  1. Click Access The Times below to open the NYT landing page.
  2. Click the black Create Account button.
  3. Enter your UND email address and click Continue.
  4. Create a password (this does not need to be your UND password).
  5. Select Student or Faculty/Staff.
  6. Finish setting up your account.
  7. Install the NYT, NYT Games, and NYT Cooking apps if you'd like.

Heads up: Your pass renews periodically — if you're prompted to sign in again, just revisit this page and click through.

The Atlantic
Of No Party or Clique

Full access to TheAtlantic.com: long-form reporting, cultural criticism, politics, science, and the complete magazine content alongside web-only features.

How to Sign In
  1. Click Access The Atlantic below.
  2. In the top-right corner of the Atlantic site, click Sign In.
  3. Select An Institution Login.
  4. Search for and select University of North Dakota.
  5. Select your UND status and enter your UND email address.
  6. Sign up for emails, or click Skip in the top-right.

Grand Forks Herald
North Dakota's Newspaper of Record

Full-text access to the Grand Forks Herald — local news, alumni mentions, UND coverage, and the community record of record. Sign in with your UND credentials and start reading.

Getting In & Finding More ND Papers
  1. Click Read the Herald below and sign in with your UND credentials if prompted.
  2. Use the site's search to find articles by keyword, date, or section.
  3. For other North Dakota papers — the Bismarck Tribune, Fargo Forum, Minot Daily News, and dozens more — use Access World News.

Tip: Access World News is excellent for local-history research and tracking how a story developed across multiple ND outlets.

Aviation Week Intelligence Network
AWIN · The Aerospace Industry's Source of Record

The complete AWIN platform: Aviation Week & Space Technology, Aviation Daily, Aerospace Daily & Defense Report, The Weekly of Business Aviation, Inside MRO, and more — with coverage going back to 1916, fleet data, program profiles, and the World Aerospace Database.

Indispensable for Aerospace Sciences, Space Studies, and anyone researching the aviation and defense industries.

Getting the Most Out of AWIN
  • Create a My AWIN account the first time you access the site to save searches, set alerts, and use some premium features.
  • Advanced Search lets you limit by Articles and filter by publication and date range — far more precise than the quick search.
  • Fleet Data & Organizations tabs profile operators, aircraft by serial number, and program status.
  • Close your browser when finished — concurrent user access is limited by our license.
  • The online edition of AW&ST is updated weekly; the print/archive edition compiles biweekly and may have different titles and photos.

On Your Phone or Tablet

Libby: Magazines & More, Wherever You Are

The Libby app — through the North Dakota Academic Consortium's OverDrive collection — gives you checkouts of popular magazines, audiobooks, and ebooks using your UND affiliation. No due dates to worry about for magazines; they simply expire when you're done.

Three Steps to Get Reading

  3. Sign in with your UND credentials and start borrowing.

Good to Know
What's in the Collection?
Magazines, Audiobooks, Ebooks

Libby's magazine collection rotates, but expect titles across news, lifestyle, hobbies, business, and entertainment. Magazines don't count against audiobook/ebook limits, and back issues are often available months after the current release.

Looking for something specific? Use the Search icon in Libby, or browse the Magazines category from the library home screen.

Can't Find What You Need?

Ask a librarian. We'll help you track down a specific article, set up a citation export, or build a saved search that emails you when new coverage appears.
